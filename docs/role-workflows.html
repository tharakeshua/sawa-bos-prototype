What changes for BBWbrand-specific role layer

BBW's Store Manager (Hagar) does not run individual targets — she tracks segment sales. BBW's role layer therefore swaps the per-associate team list for the SFA011 hourly segment table, surfaces Aura Quick + Full enrolment on the Today screen, and lets her scan a SKU to see floor + backstore quantity in one tap. Every other brand keeps the standard role surface.

Aura panel

today · live

Quick enrol · Full enrol · Linkage value · capture rate. Daily, not weekly — fixes the manual-cashier problem Hagar named in the call.

Segment sales · SFA011

team · live

Hourly segments with Plan · Act · LY · Trans · ATV · IPC · Footfall · Conv · Mix. Live indicator on the active segment.

Scan stock

stock · live

Type or scan a SKU → instant card showing floor / backstore / total. Works on iPad and iPhone, offline-tolerant.
