Solution 4 20 · brand-personal
Solution · ready target · break-down by segment

Hagar asked for a ready target.
So we built one.

The system target was unrealistic — last year's SAS, this year's empty port. Hagar overrides every morning anyway. The cockpit pre-fills the override — same promotion, same weekday, last comparable week — and lets her tweak in one tap.

  • Same-promo, last-comparable-week baseline — not last year
  • Segment break-down auto-derived — segments 1–4 (every 2 hrs)
  • One-tap edit by Store Mgr — saved into the morning DCO
  • Promotion calendar aware — flags SAS, BOGO, 5-for-150 conflicts
  • Footfall trend · not money trend — Hagar's exact ask
Solution 5 · Hagar's ask 21 · ready target
Solution · drillable channels

Five channels.
One funnel.

Endless Aisle was a metric in search of an owner — we merged it into Assisted Ecom across all seven brands. The funnel reads cleaner. Hadrin orders, MPOS sales, SAWA POS receipts — all attributed without copy-paste.

  • SAWA POS · MPOS · Hadrin · Asst. Ecom · SCO
  • Tap a card to drill — every channel opens its own deeper view
  • Auto-attribution — no manual scanning, zero data conflicts
  • Endless Aisle merged — its volume rolls into Assisted Ecom
  • Offline graceful — SCO greys out cleanly when not deployed
